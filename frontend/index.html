The Living Buddhist Heritage of Sikkim

Nestled in the eastern Himalayas, Sikkim is home to some of the world's most remarkable Buddhist monasteries. From the grandeur of Rumtek — seat of the Kagyu order — to the solitary sanctity of Dubdi perched above Yuksom, each monastery tells a unique story.

Monastery360 was built to bridge distances, preserve culture, and inspire reverence for these magnificent places of worship through modern technology.
